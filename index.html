01The transformation arc

Four years from a founder's instinct to a repeatable engine.

The clearest way to see the change is the evolution of how Novobi sells and delivers — the spine that everything else hangs from.

Pre-2022

Founder-led & ad hoc

Sales depended on the founders. Most revenue came from rescue projects — fixing other firms' failed implementations.

2022–2023

First dedicated sellers

Sellers hired and revenue shifted toward net-new implementations — but sellers coordinated and negotiated rather than consulted.

2024–Present

Consultant-led model

A consultative sales motion and industry "Practices" established. The people who understand delivery now lead the deal.

Inflection point
Designed for next

Client Partners + standalone Delivery

A defined Client Partnership sales team and a broken-out Delivery org — clean mandates, clean metrics, no handoff cliffs.

In motion
02Eight operating systems, built

Where there were habits, I built structure.

Each of these was an area run on individual judgment and goodwill. Each is now a defined system — with owners, standards, and numbers. The pattern is consistent: institutionalize what used to live in a few people's heads.

/ 01

Account Management

Turning one-off projects into long-term operating partnerships and a recurring-revenue flywheel.

FromRelationships managed informally; support sold after go-live, when leverage was already gone. Recurring revenue was an afterthought.
ToA defined lifecycle — land → implement → adopt → optimize → expand → renew — with recurring services packaged up front, a four-tier services catalog aligned to client maturity, and explicit account-ownership policies.
Recurring Revenue Flywheel 4-Pillar Services Catalog Quarterly Governance Reviews Account Ownership Policy
/ 02

Sales

From founder dependency to a disciplined, consultative commercial function.

FromNo clear sales mandate or metrics. Lead distribution was ad hoc, inviting conflict; sellers got pulled into delivery and lost capacity to sell the next deal.
ToA Client Partnership team with a North Star — "win the right deals, the right way, and keep clients long-term." Every deal carries an ROI story, clear scope and risk posture, and handoff artifacts that set Delivery up to succeed.
Deal ROI Framework Discount Approval Matrix Qualification Standards Blueprint-Fit Rate 70%+
/ 03

Delivery

Engineering an execution machine that holds up when the company doubles, not one that depends on a few exceptional people.

FromHero-driven outcomes. Success depended on heroics, performance varied by region, knowledge wasn't institutionalized, and teams were often in firefighting mode.
ToRepeatable, AI-enabled delivery pods with a clear RACI, a standardized A&D and implementation process, industry blueprints and templates, and Centers of Excellence that own capability maturity across the org.
POD Playbook Centers of Excellence PMO Practice Industry Blueprints AI-First Leverage
/ 04

Utilization & KPIs

Making capacity and performance measurable, so growth decisions rest on data instead of gut.

FromCapacity was a feeling. There was no consistent view of who was over- or under-loaded, and no standard for what "fully utilized" meant by role.
ToBillable-utilization targets by level (75% at junior levels, stepping down with management load), a tracked bench % with 10–20% as the healthy band, and a target of >85% external utilization maintained even while hiring.
Utilization Targets by Level Bench % Thresholds Sales · Delivery · Marketing KPI Reporting Utilization Bonus Structure
/ 05

Hiring & Performance Management

Replacing reactive recruiting and uneven reviews with workforce planning and one consistent standard.

FromReactive hiring with no predictability. Workforce planning was non-existent, onboarding was manager-dependent, there was no management training, and performance management was inconsistent.
ToRevenue-driven hiring — approved only against secured revenue or validated pipeline, sized by a weighted-demand model W(CRM) = Σ(E × P) and a deal-to-hiring EBITDA guardrail. A formal performance cycle (plan → monitor → 360 review → reward) scored against Operating Principles, plus a new Director of People mandate and competency frameworks.
W(CRM) Demand Model Deal-to-Hiring Guardrail 360° Review Process Manager Development Program Competency Matrix
/ 06

Forecasting & Budgeting

Connecting the pipeline, the resource plan, and the P&L into one governed system.

FromPlanning was disconnected — pipeline, staffing, and budget lived in different places, so forecasts didn't translate into hiring or margin decisions.
ToA 12-month rolling revenue forecast built from contracted plus probability-weighted pipeline, converted on a monthly/quarterly cadence into budget — all governed by a 10% EBITDA run-rate guardrail on hiring and discretionary spend, with regular resource-allocation auditing.
12-Month Rolling Forecast CRM → Budget Conversion 10% EBITDA Guardrail PSA Allocation Auditing
/ 07

Moving Upmarket

Repositioning Novobi from small rescue work toward mid-market and enterprise ERP transformation.

FromA book built largely on rescue projects and smaller engagements, with clients hard to segment and a value story told in features and hours.
ToA data-backed go-to-market — 68 clients analyzed into three operating verticals, a TAM/market-sizing study, and industry blueprints (Multi-Channel Retail, ETO Manufacturing) targeting $150K+ Phase 1 deals. The flagship VIB program — 365 mapped requirements, multi-plant scope — proves the firm can run enterprise-grade engagements, timed to Odoo's own move up the value chain.
Odoo NA Market Sizing 3-Vertical Segmentation VIB Enterprise Program $150K+ Blueprint Deals Strategic M&A Readiness
/ 08

Consultative Capability

Adding business-consulting muscle on top of strong functional and technical implementation.

FromDeep engineering strength, but weak consulting capability — sellers coordinated and negotiated, and the conversation started with software, not the client's business outcome.
ToAn eight-pillar A&D analysis framework (engagement alignment, business, functional, technical, data, adoption, governance, commercial), a structured Architect & Design entry point, blueprint SME rosters, and a consultative North Star — every conversation starts with business impact and ROI, not features and hours.
8-Pillar A&D Framework Architect & Design Entry Point Solution Consulting Playbooks Expert Consulting Practice

The goal was never a better quarter. It was a delivery engine that works when the company doubles — reinforced by systems, not dependent on a few exceptional individuals.
